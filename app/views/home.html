Report a smell from Walleys Quarry landfill site

Walleys Quarry Landfill Site Incident Reporting Form

The Environment Agency provides weekly updates on Walleys Quarry via its Citizen Space website (opens in new window).

To subscribe to receive these updates directly, please contact the West Midlands Enquiries Team at: Enquiries_WestMids@environment-agency.gov.uk. You can unsubscribe at any time.

If you still wish to report the smell from Walleys Quarry after reading these updates, please use this data capture form.

Completing the short set of questions within this form will help us to understand how Walleys Quarry landfill site affects you.

All reports are logged, and a unique reference number will be generated for each report. This number will be sent to you immediately after completion of this form, if you provide us with a valid email address.
